Getting Started
Account Setup
How do I create a DilFit account?

Download DilFit from the App Store or Google Play. Tap Sign Up and choose to register with your email address, Google account, or Apple ID. Follow the onboarding steps to set your fitness goals and preferences.

I forgot my password — how do I reset it?

On the login screen, tap Forgot Password. Enter your registered email address and we'll send you a reset link. The link expires after 1 hour.

If you signed up via Google or Apple, use those buttons to log in — no password is needed.

Can I use DilFit without an internet connection?

Yes. DilFit caches your workout history and exercise library locally so you can log workouts offline. Your data syncs automatically the next time you connect to the internet.

How do I set my fitness goals and unit preferences?

Go to Profile → Settings → Fitness Preferences. Here you can set:

  • Weight unit (kg / lbs)
  • Weekly workout goal
  • Primary fitness objective (strength, muscle gain, weight loss, endurance)
  • Experience level
Workouts & Tracking
Logging Workouts
How do I log a workout?

Tap the + button on the home screen or navigate to the Workout tab and tap Start Workout. You can:

  • Search from 700+ exercises in our library
  • Add sets, reps, and weight for each exercise
  • Start a rest timer between sets
  • Add notes for any exercise

When done, tap Finish Workout to save it.

Can I create and save custom workout templates?

Yes. After building a workout, tap Save as Template. Your templates appear in the Templates tab so you can quickly start the same routine again.

How do I track my personal records (PRs)?

DilFit automatically detects new PRs when you log a set that beats your previous best. You'll see a 🏆 trophy badge on the set. Visit Profile → Progress to see all your PRs by exercise.

How do I edit or delete a past workout?

Go to History, find the workout date, and tap on it. Tap the ⋯ menu in the top-right corner to edit or delete the entry.

Why is the rest timer not working?

Ensure Background App Refresh is enabled for DilFit in iOS Settings → DilFit. Also check that notifications are allowed, as the timer alert requires notification permissions.

Account & Billing
Subscription
What's included in DilFit Pro?

DilFit Pro unlocks:

  • Advanced progress analytics and body measurements
  • Priority customer support
  • Custom workout plan generation
  • Create and manage private challenges

Free users still get full workout tracking, the social feed, challenges, and essential progress insights.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through the App Store or Google Play — not directly through DilFit.

  • iOS: Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → DilFit → Cancel
  • Android: Google Play → Profile icon → Payments → Subscriptions → DilFit → Cancel

You'll retain Pro access until the end of your current billing period.

I was charged but my account is still on the free plan.

This is usually a sync delay. Pull down to refresh on the Profile screen. If it still shows free after 5 minutes, tap Profile → Settings → Restore Purchases. Still not working? Email us at bek.develop@gmail.com with your order confirmation.

Data & Privacy
How do I export my workout data?

Go to Profile → Settings → Export Data. You'll receive a CSV file with your full workout history via email within a few minutes.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account at any time:

  • In-app: Profile → Settings → Delete Account
  • By email: Send a deletion request to bek.develop@gmail.com from your registered address

⚠ This action is permanent

Deleting your account permanently removes all your workouts, social posts, challenges, badges, and profile data within 30 days. This cannot be undone. Consider exporting your data first.

Social & Challenges
How do I follow someone on DilFit?

Tap on a user's profile photo or name anywhere in the app to open their profile. Tap the Follow button. If their account is private, they'll need to approve your request.

How do challenges work?

Challenges are group fitness competitions you can join or create. Navigate to the Challenges tab, browse active challenges, and tap Join. Progress is tracked automatically from your workout logs. Leaderboards update in real time.

How do I report inappropriate content?

Long-press any post or comment and tap Report. Our moderation team reviews all reports within 24 hours. For urgent safety issues, email bek.develop@gmail.com.

How do I make my profile private?

Go to Profile → Settings → Privacy → Private Account. With a private account, only approved followers can see your posts and workout history.
